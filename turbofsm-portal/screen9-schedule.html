📝 Schedule — Month View Features:
  • Event Times: Each event shows its scheduled time before the type and customer name (e.g., "9:00 AM · Appt: Smith").
  • Clickable Events: Click any event badge to navigate to its detail page (Appointments → Appt Detail, Jobs → Job Detail).
  • Drag & Drop Rescheduling: Hover over any event to see the grip handle (⋮⋮). Drag the event to another day to reschedule. A confirmation tooltip appears on the target day.
  • Demo: The blue dashed border on Mar 5 shows the "drag source" state. The green dashed border on Mar 12 shows the "drop target" state. The semi-transparent event on Mar 5 shows the "being dragged" state.
  • View Toggle: Use Day / Week / Month buttons to switch calendar views. Each opens a separate page.
  • Recurring Jobs: Events prefixed with 🔄 indicate visits from a recurring job (e.g., "🔄 10:00 AM · Lawn: Park" for weekly lawn service on Tuesdays). Individual visits still appear as separate calendar events even though they are grouped into billing periods on the Jobs page.
