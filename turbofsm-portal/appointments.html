📝 Appointment List Page: Displays all appointments with their statuses and chain relationships.

Status-specific actions:
• Pending / Cancelled: View Details + Mark as Completed + SMS
• Completed: View Details + Create Estimate + View Notes + Follow-up + Create Job

Follow-up Chains: When a follow-up is created, the parent appointment automatically becomes "Completed" and the sub-appointment sits directly below with a teal left border + ↳ arrow indent. Parent shows "🔗 1 of N". See: Sarah Johnson (Site Survey → Follow Up) and Lisa Brown (Introduction → Follow Up).

Navigation: "+ Create Appointment" opens the form (Screen 8a). "View" opens the detail page (Screen 8b).
