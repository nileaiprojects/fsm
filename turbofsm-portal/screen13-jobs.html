📝 Job List Page — Billing-Period-Based Recurring Model: Shows all jobs including recurring job support. Jobs are auto-created when estimates are approved, or manually via "+ Create Job".

Recurring Job Features (Billing-Period Model):
• Each recurring "job" on this list represents one billing period (not an individual visit)
• Parent row shows: frequency, billing cycle, and progress (e.g., "Weekly · Monthly Billing · Job 3 of 12")
• Child rows show billing periods with visit counts (e.g., "Mar 2026 (4 visits)") and visit completion status
• Quote column shows invoice amount per job = rate per visit × visits per billing period (e.g., $300 × 4 = $1,200/job)
• Start/End dates reflect the billing period date range (e.g., Mar 1 – Mar 31)
• "Active" status badge (teal) indicates an ongoing recurrence series
• Filter options: "Recurring Only" and "Active Recurring" in the status dropdown
