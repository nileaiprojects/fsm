📝 Invoicing — Billing-Period-Based Recurring Model:
  • Billing-Period Invoices: Each recurring invoice corresponds to one billing period (e.g., "Job #3 of 12 · Mar 2026 · 4 visits × $300"). The invoice amount = rate per visit × visits in the billing period.
  • Billing Context Banner: When creating an invoice for a recurring job, a teal banner shows the billing period number, visit count, rate per visit, and calculated total (e.g., "4 visits × $300/visit = $1,200").
  • Batch Invoice: A purple banner offers to create invoices for all completed billing periods in one action (e.g., "1 completed billing period found for JOB-0007").
  • Auto-Calculated Amount: Invoice amount fields show Rate per Visit, Visits in Billing Period, and auto-calculated Invoice Amount (readonly).
  • Job Dropdown: Recurring jobs in the dropdown show billing period context (e.g., "JOB-0007 - David Park - Lawn Service (🔄 Job #3 · Mar 2026 · 4 visits)").
  • Filter: "Recurring Only" filter option in status dropdown to isolate recurring job invoices.
