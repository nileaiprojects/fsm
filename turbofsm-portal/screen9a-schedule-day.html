  • Vertical Timeline: Shows hours from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM with 1-hour slots (80px each).
  • Event Blocks: Colored blocks span their time duration, showing time range, type badge, customer name, assigned crew member with initials avatar, and address.
  • Overlapping Events: When events overlap, they display side-by-side in columns.
  • Clickable: Click any event block to navigate to its detail page (Appointment Detail or Job Detail).
  • Navigation: Use ◀/▶ arrows to move between days. Use Day/Week/Month toggle to switch views.
  • Recurring Jobs: Event blocks for recurring jobs show a 🔄 prefix in the type label (e.g., "🔄 Job Schedule") to distinguish them from one-time jobs. Each visit within a billing period appears as an individual event on the calendar.
